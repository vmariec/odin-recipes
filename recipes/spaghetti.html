Spaghetti Recipe

Easy and Delicious Spaghetti Recipe and Instructions

This spaghetti recipe is quick and easy. It takes about 30 minutes to complete and will be enough to feed to whole family. The delicious taste will have you coming back for seconds. This is great recipes for those night you come home tired but want to make sure your tummy is sastiied or those pot lucks that you need a good, easy recipe to make and bring along, and much more!

Ingredients:

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 16oz jar of meat sauce or any sauce you prefer
  • 1lb of ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 package of spaghetti pasta
  • Dried Oregano to taste
  • Salt for flavor
  • Pepper for flavor
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. First, you want to turn your stove onto low-medium heat and place a large hot pan on the stove. Once your pan is heated up, melted the butter in it and saute the onions until they are translucent.
  2. Second, you will want to grab another large pan that will fit enough water to cook your desired pasta and let that water boil until we are ready to use it.
  3. After, you are going to add the ground beef into the pan and season with salt, papper, and oregano. Cook thoroughly.
  4. Once, your meat it fully cooked, you'll want to add the marinara or meat sauce into the pan and let that simmer on low heat.
  5. While everything else is simmering together, you can head back to the other large pan filled with what should be boiling water by now and go ahead and cook your pasta following the instructions. *make sure to keep about a cup of pasta water
  6. Lastly, once the pasta is cooked, go on ahead and add the noodles into the meat sauce along with that cup of pasta water and voila. Your quick and delicious spaghetti is ready to serve. Feel free to top it off with any cheese or add some garlic bread into the mix.

Scrambled Egg Recipe

Recipe for Delicious Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Start by heating up a pan on low-medium heat
  2. While the pan is warming up, you will want to crack the 2 eggs in a bowl and whisk thoroughly.
  3. Once the pan is ready, melt some butter in it and drop in the mixed eggs.
  4. Consistently stir the eggs in the pan until it has reached the level of consistency you desire.
  5. After, plate the eggs and top them off with salt and pepper for extra flavor. Enjoy!

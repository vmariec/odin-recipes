Salmon Recipe

Delicious Salmon and Broccoli Meal

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon filets
  • Lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Broccoli

Steps:

  1. First, you will want to preheat your oven to 400 degrees.
  2. While the oven is heating up, on a pan, coat the salmon with oil and lemon juice, then sprinkle on salt and pepper to taste as desired.
  3. After, on the same pan, coat the broccoli in oil and add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Once the oven is ready, throw the pan in and leave it in for 15 minutes. If you want to leave the salmon in for a little longer feel free, but once you take it out it should be ready to serve. Enjoy!
